✨ How to Play Tic Tac Twist! ✨

Welcome to a game of Tic-Tac-Toe with a wild twist! The goal is simple:Get three of your pieces in a row.

The Game ✏️

  • Players take turns placing their 'X' or 'O' in an empty square.
  • The first player to get three in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) wins!

The Twist! 🏇

If the board fills up and no one has won, the game isn't over. It's time forKnight Mode!

  • No More Placing:You can no longer place new pieces. Instead, you mustmoveone of your pieces already on the board.
  • Knight's Move:Moves must be in an'L' shape--just like a knight in chess (two squares in one direction, then one square perpendicular).
  • Path Clearing:As your piece moves, anyotherpieces (yours or your opponent's) on the 1 or 2 squares it "jumps over" areremoved from the board!

Knight Mode Landing Rules 🛬

What happens when your piece lands depends on the square:

  • Land on an EMPTY square:Your piece moves there. Your starting square becomes empty.
  • Land on an OPPONENT'S piece:Your piece and the opponent's pieceswap places.
  • Land on your OWN piece:Your two piecesswap places, BUT your starting square iscleared(it becomes empty). This is a "self-clearing" move!

How to Win 🏆

You can win at any time, duringThe GameorThe Twist!The first player to get three of their pieces in a row wins the game.

Good luck, and think ahead!
